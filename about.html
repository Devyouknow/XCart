About Xcart

Xcart is a smart shopping cart designed to solve the problem of hassle-free shopping. With Xcart, customers can easily scan items, generate their bills, and pay using various online payment systems like PayPal, Google Pay, or UPI.

Our vision is to help store owners maintain a cashier-free environment while enhancing the shopping experience for customers. Xcart is currently in its development phase and is revolutionizing grocery shopping, especially in India.
